Privacy notice

Last updated: 14 July 2026.

The short version

DecorPalette runs entirely in your browser. It does not collect personal data, does not use tracking cookies, and does not send your palettes to any server.

What is stored, and where

  • Saved palettes are stored in your browser's localStorage on your own device.
  • Theme preference is stored the same way so the app remembers your choice.

This data never leaves your device. Clearing your browser's site data removes it permanently.

Analytics and third parties

The core application loads no third-party scripts, fonts, or analytics. If you self-host a fork and add such services, update this notice accordingly.

Your control

You can delete any saved palette from within the app, or clear all stored data through your browser settings at any time.

Contact

Questions about privacy or security can be sent to security@decorpalette.app.
